Our Policies

Learn about Nova Labs' commitment to safety, ethics, and responsible innovation in robotics and automation.

Privacy Policy

At Nova Labs, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Project data and technical specifications
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve user experience
  • To communicate important updates
  • To ensure platform security

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored on secure servers and accessed only by authorized personnel.

Safety & Ethics Policy

Nova Labs is dedicated to promoting safe and ethical practices in robotics and automation development.

Safety Guidelines

  • All projects must include proper safety documentation
  • Risk assessments are required for all experimental work
  • Emergency protocols must be established and practiced
  • Proper personal protective equipment must be used

Ethical Standards

  • Respect for human dignity and rights
  • Transparency in AI and automation systems
  • Accountability for system behavior
  • Fairness and non-discrimination in algorithms

Compliance

All Nova Labs projects must comply with relevant local, national, and international regulations governing robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence.

Community Guidelines

Our community is built on principles of collaboration, respect, and innovation.

Code of Conduct

  • Treat all members with respect and professionalism
  • Share knowledge and help others learn
  • Give proper credit for others' work
  • Maintain a harassment-free environment

Project Collaboration

  • Open communication and regular updates
  • Clear documentation of work
  • Respect for intellectual property rights
  • Constructive feedback and peer review

Enforcement

Violations of community guidelines may result in temporary or permanent suspension of access to Nova Labs resources and facilities.

Intellectual Property Policy

Protecting intellectual property while fostering innovation is central to our mission.

Ownership Rights

  • Individual creators retain rights to their original work
  • Collaborative projects require clear ownership agreements
  • Open source contributions follow respective license terms
  • Nova Labs claims no ownership of member-created content

Licensing

  • Choose appropriate licenses for your projects
  • Respect third-party intellectual property
  • Document all external dependencies
  • Follow open source contribution guidelines

Commercialization

Members are encouraged to commercialize their innovations, with Nova Labs providing support through mentorship and resources while respecting individual ownership rights.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

For questions about our policies, please contact: policies@novalabs.org

Have Questions About Our Policies?

Our team is here to help clarify any policy questions and ensure you have the information you need to work safely and effectively within our community.
